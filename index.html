Remote worker? This might be for you...

Do you work online? Tired of the same old restaurants and work spaces? Make this boat your office for a week and cruise around Phuket without losing wages. Welcome to Office Cruises Phuket.

Wake up every day in a beautiful new anchorage next to the amazing wild beaches and Scenery that surrounds Phuket. Then work your passage by doing your normal online job in the comfort and serenity of your new floating office.

6 day cruises for $450 per double cabin, (why not share with your partner to add romance and share costs). Food included, trips to shore included, snorkelling gear included.

    Bavaria 39 Monohull 2010

    Launched in 2010, this Cruiser 39 has been very well kept by the owner. She joined our charter fleet in 2021. She offers a very spacious owners section in the bow with ensuite bathroom while the two aft dbl cabins share one more toilet with seperate shower compartment. Nicely equipped with watermaker, TV/DVD, furling mainsail, bimini, dinghy davits, solar panels, GPS chart plotter at the helm and autopilot.
